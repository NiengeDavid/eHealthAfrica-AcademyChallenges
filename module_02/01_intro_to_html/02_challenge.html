Nature's Beauty!

In the meadow, flowers bloom so bright,
Underneath the warm and golden light.
A gentle breeze whispers through the trees,
Nature's song, carried on the gentlest breeze.

The world awakens with the morning dew,
A canvas of colors, a stunning view.
Birds above in the endless sky,
Their joyful chorus, oh so high.

As day turns into the peaceful night,
Stars above, a celestial sight.
In this world so vast and grand,
I find my peace, I understand.

Simple moments, beauty untold,
In nature's arms, my heart takes hold.
For in these wonders, great and small,
I find the greatest gift of all.
